Partnership

Strong relationships and networks have existed for several years between Ireland and Cambodia. 2020 and 2021 have provided a new opportunity to further strengthen these bonds. Leaders from the Cambodian Ministry of Education and SeeBeyondBorders staff took part in a study and collaboration visit to Ireland. This provided an opportunity to engage our Irish network and develop new collaborations. Our aim now is to further these connections.
