Testimonials
Once basic and home needs are satisfied, access to a fully trained, well resourced teacher is the single most important ingredient for childhood flourishing. As fellow educators, we need to embrace our colleagues in Cambodia. They have suffered an absolute loss of pedagogy. However, it can be recovered through appropriate training and support. SeeBeyondBorders is creating positive, systemic and sustainable change

Dr. Leo Casey - Director of Teaching and Learning at the National College of Ireland

We visited Ireland with SeeBeyondBorders in 2020. That trip really touched my heart and inspired me to work more closely together with SeeBeyondBorders to improve Cambodian education

Chan Koulika Director of Policy, Cambodian Ministry of Education

As an Irish company with a Cambodian footprint we know firsthand how vital it is to improve education for children in Cambodia. Our investment in SeeBeyondBorders delivers for Cambodian children. By transforming teachers we are reaching far more children. We call on others to follow our lead.

Tom McGuinness, Founder of Horseware
