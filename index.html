Why We Exist
SeeBeyondBorders Ireland is a newly established charity aiming to build an Irish footprint in support of inclusive education for all Cambodian children. We will support the work SeeBeyondBorders is doing on the ground in rural Cambodia to provide quality education at school.

Message from our Chairperson
“We believe passionately in the power of education to empower people and enact change. SeeBeyondBorders has been working on the ground in Cambodia since 2009 and it is wonderful to be now launching here in Ireland. Our work is to fundraise and support the programmes that SeeBeyondBorders implements in rural Cambodian schools. Improving learning for Cambodian children is the focus of our work. Programmes are all evidence-based and have proven success. Your support is very welcome.”
