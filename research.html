Research

SeeBeyondBorders prides itself on using evidence-based programmes to deliver quality teaching and learning to Cambodia children. Here in Ireland our organisation has developed strong links with various educational institutions including schools, universities and teaching organisations.
Conversations on Education Inequality Webinar

On July 7th 2021 we co-hosted the Webinar
'Conversations about the impact of inequality on education: Spotlight on Cambodia'
in cooperation with Dublin City University and the CambodiaIreland Changemaker Network.

VIDEO
Dr. Bo Chankoulika - Head of Department of Policy, Cambodia

Dr. Bo Chankoulika discusses the research from the Department of Policy in the time of COVID-19 and the impacts on education in Cambodia.
VIDEO
Pheung Pov -
Country Manager,
SeeBeyondBorders

Pheung Pov shares his experience of growing up in the Cambodian education system and the challenges children, families and communities throughout Cambodia face.

Prof. Mathias Urban - DCU

Prof. Mathias Urban outlines how education can tackle inequality and the importance of connections to the early childhood systems.
